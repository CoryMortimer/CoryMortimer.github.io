About Me


Just Your Typical Drama and Software Engineering Double Major


I was born and raised in a small town (of around 3,000 people at the time) in Wisconsin. It was quite an experience, but I soon realized I needed to be in a larger city. That's how I ventured out to California.

I love to hang out with friends and family, work on little programming projects, and improv in my spare time. I'm currently on Improv Revolution and it is a fun time! Come check out some of our free shows!

FLOQ: Fun Loving. Organized. Quirky. FLOQ are the characteristics that most describe me.
